\section{{\tt STATUS} command}
\index{status command}
\index{utilization}
\index{memory}
%------------------------------------------------------------------------
\subsection{Syntax}
\begin{verse}
{\tt status} \\
{\tt status notime}
\end{verse}
%------------------------------------------------------------------------
\subsection{Purpose}

Shows information on how the system resources are being utilized.

The {\tt notime} option omits times but prints everything else.
